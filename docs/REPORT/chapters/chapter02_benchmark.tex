\chapter{Benchmarking}
This chapter presents the Robodog platform under the flag of quadruped robot projects. The benchmark was conducted to establish repeatable baseline metrics and to identify performance before field deployement. Results are compared against reference figures from the Spot-Micro community, against Boston Dynamics specifications published in the open literature and five other representative platforms across hardware architecture, actuation, software stack, opennes, cost and use cases, providing th reader with a clear picture of where the Robodog sits and what trade-offs were made in its design.
